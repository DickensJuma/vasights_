We are a research firm based in Nairobi, Kenya, providing technical expertise in data science, research, and GIS.

Our Services

Data Science

Our experts can help you automate your data science workflow. We have more than 5 years of industry experience in automating reports using technologies such as R-markdown and power BI.
We also build powerful dashboards for data visualization using javascript, python, and R (Flexi and shiny dashboard).
We integrate this software with data collection software such as REDCap and ODK to enable real-time data visualization.

Research

We link researchers across Africa to competent statisticians;
There is a need to build up collaboration between researchers and statisticians on the African continent.
We bridge this gap by connecting researchers to first-class statisticians.
This ensures that researchers obtain statistical support at the early stages of their research which improves the study design and overall quality of the research.

G.I.S

Spatial Analysis, Cartographic Services, Remote Sensing and Capacity Building;
Location intelligence is a key component of decision making in businesses and organizations.
Our experts understand the application of GIS in different business environments.
With advanced mapping and spatial analysis techniques, we provide spatially informed analytics that helps businesses make smart decisions.
Our key aim among others is to help organizations monitor resources and performance which improves efficiency and overall business growth.

Capacity Building

We offer training and capacity building in data science, GIS, and research;
We train your analysts on how to use tools like R and Python for data science, creating powerful dashboards using shiny in R, and automating reports using R markdown.
We also offer training on the use of ODK, KoBoToolbox, REDCap for data collection and how these technologies can be integrated with others using APIs.
